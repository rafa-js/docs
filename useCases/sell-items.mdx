---
title: Sell Items in Handcash Shop
description: 'Selling Items in the Handcash Shop'
---

To promote your game in the Handcash Market, create a payment request that represents a game item. Once the payment request is paid, your application will receive a webhook and issue items directly to the buyer.

Contact sales@handcash.io for assistance and to get your game items listed on the market.
